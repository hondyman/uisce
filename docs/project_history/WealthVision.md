# WealthVision: Strategic Enhancements & Roadmap

## Executive Summary
This document outlines strategic enhancements to elevate the semlayer wealth management platform beyond current market leaders (Addepar, Black Diamond, Orion) and position it as the industry-leading solution for ultra-high-net-worth (UHNW) wealth management.

**Strengths**: Billing, reporting, large user base
