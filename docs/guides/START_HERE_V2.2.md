## What Is This?

A **semantic-driven entity field builder** where:
- ✅ Fields come from semantic catalog (not manual entry)
- ✅ Names auto-populate from catalog
- ✅ Types auto-populate from catalog
- ✅ Full CRUD + reordering
- ✅ Inherited fields protected (locked)
- ✅ 4x faster than before

---

## The 3-Step Workflow

```
Step 1: Click entity in sidebar
        ↓
Step 2: Click [+Add Field] button
        ↓
Step 3: Select semantic term from modal
        ↓
Result: Field instantly added, auto-populated
```

## Quick Q&A

**Q: Is this backward compatible?**  
A: Yes! 100% backward compatible. Old schemas still work.

**Q: What if I break something?**  
A: Inherited fields are locked (protected). You can only edit your assigned fields.

**Q: How do I add a field?**  
A: Click [+Add], search semantic term, select, done. Takes 30 seconds.

**Q: Can I reorder fields?**  
A: Yes! Click up/down buttons. Changes persist when you save.
